# Blog Strategy roadmap

- **BS-01 — Client Blog Inventory and Organization Foundation:** projects, client context, manual and
  bounded evidence intake, classifications, topic families, manual overlap review, decisions,
  approvals, readiness, and one-sheet export.
- **BS-02 — Deterministic Cannibalization Analysis:** weighted candidates, groups, evidence, review,
  preferred pages, decisions, and rerun history.
- **BS-03 — OpenAI-Assisted Classification:** structured classification, prompt versions, caching,
  suggestions, and human approval.
- **BS-04 — Competitor Blog Research:** typed competitors, inventories, normalized topics, and crawl
  completeness.
- **BS-05 — ChatGPT and Claude Research Reconciliation:** prompts, imports, evidence, claim classes,
  agreement/conflict review, and verification queue.
- **BS-06 — Opportunity Matrix and Final Blog Strategy:** coverage comparison, opportunities,
  priority, safeguards, and final roadmap export.

These identifiers are separate from the existing sitemap/crawl numbered roadmap and must never
rename, reuse, or reinterpret its phase numbers.
